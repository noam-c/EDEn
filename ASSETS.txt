Below is a list of all third-party art assets currently used by EDEn, as well as the artists responsible and their websites.

Audio assets:
Title theme - Schroeder's Failure, by trash80 (http://www.trash80.net).
Menu sound (reselect) - Interface Beeps, by Bart Kelsey (http://www.opengameart.org/users/bart)
Menu sound (choose) - Random SFX, by Écrivain (http://www.opengameart.org/users/Écrivain)

Art assets:
Menu background - In the Deep, by darkwood67 (http://darkwood67.deviantart.com)
Title background - Fields, by darkwood67 (http://darkwood67.deviantart.com)
NPC 1 sprite - 2D RPG character, by arikel (http://opengameart.org/users/arikel)
Kain sprite - Final Fantasy Tactics Squire sprite, by Sithjester (http://untamed.wild-refuge.net/)
Menu portraits - Kain, Shiara, and Akrom, by Noam Chitayat (http://github.com/noam-c)
Town tileset - Town Tiles, by surt (http://opengameart.org/content/town-tiles)
Item and Skill icons - 420 Pixel Art Icons for RPG, by Ails (http://ails.deviantart.com)
